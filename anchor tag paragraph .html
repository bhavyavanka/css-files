service page

A service page is a webpage that provides information about a specific service or product offered by a business or organization. It typically includes details about the service or product, such as its features, benefits, pricing, and other relevant information 123.

Service pages are an essential component of a website as they help businesses to showcase their offerings and provide potential customers with the necessary information to make informed decisions. A well-designed service page can help build trust and credibility with the audience and increase the chances of conversion 45.
